What is Intellectual Revoulution?

A period of significant advancements in science, technology, and ideas that change people's beliefs and perceptions.

Showed how society was transformed by the science and technology.

The intellectual revolution refers to a period of significant change in the way people think and acquire knowledge.

Intellectual revolutions can also refer to changes in social and moral philosophy.

The "Intellectual Revolution" is a term which is used for referring to Greek belief about "nature" in the era before Socrates which was approximately 600 to 400 BCE.
